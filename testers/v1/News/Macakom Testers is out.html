Macakom Testers is out


Posted on: 21.7.2024.

As promised, Macakom Testers came out with mDesktop 1.4! Testers will help improve existing and upcoming software and updates before they are released to the general public. Almost anyone can become a tester, only requirement is to have Macakom ID which you can get on our Discord server and over there you can also request to join Macakom Testers.
